Mission Statement

Eliminating scalping one ticket at a time.

Vita Tickets a user friendly ticket development for your financial ticketing service needs. We provide the convience for layaway in your ticket purchasing experience to save you time and money. Our goal in this company is to eliminate second hand ticket vending that is over-priced to the consumer at face vaule.

Started in Metro Detroit with just an idea in mind and a care for the customer; we at Vita Ticket strive for a quality experience in using our services. To learn more about the team and the developers please scroll down and click on each team members face.
